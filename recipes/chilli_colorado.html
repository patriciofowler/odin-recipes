Chilli Colorado

Chilli Colorado is a flavorful dish made from beef brisket, dried chillies, and spices. It will warm you on those cool fall days or cold winter days.

I like to eat my chilli colorado over rice, but they make great tacos as well.

Ingredients

  • Beef brisket
  • Guajillo Chilli Peppers
  • Morita Chilli Peppers
  • Ancho Chilli Peppers
  • Beef Stock
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the dried chilli pepper sauce
    1. Remove seeds from the dried chilli peppers
    2. Add peppers to pan and just cover with water
    3. Bring water to boil then cover and turn off heat
    4. Let peppers sit for fifteen minutes
    5. Transfer the chilli peppers, a cup of water used to hydrate, and onion to a blender
    6. Blend until smooth
  2. Prepare the beef
    1. Cut the brisket into half inch pieces
    2. Add spices and a teaspoon of flour to the brisket and mix
    3. Bring two tablespoons of oil to a shimmer in a large pot
    4. Brown the brisket in small batches to brown in the pot
    5. Transfer the browned piece to a bowl and continue to brown other piece until done
    6. Once all the brisket is browned deglaze the pot with a bit of beef stock
    7. Add brisket back to the pot along with the chilli pepper sauce
    8. Add the beef stock and spices
    9. Bring to a simmer and cook for two hour or until the beef is tender
    10. Adjust the consistency of the chilli colorado to your liking
    11. Serve over rice or make tacos. Enjoy!
