Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna

This top rated lasagna recipe is sure to be your go to for dinner. Although lasagna is a bit of a process to put together, you and your guests will be amazed by this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Large onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the Noodles
    1. Boil water with salt
    2. Add lasagna noodles
    3. Drain water from noodles
  2. Make the Sauce
    1. Brown the sausage and beef
    2. Add onion and garlic
    3. Add tomatoes, suace, and paste
    4. Add teaspoon of sugar
  3. Assemble and cook
    1. Add a bottom layer of noodles to your
    2. Spoon a thin layer of sauce over noodles
    3. Sprinkle a thin layer of cheese over the sauce
    4. Repeat the steps until you have used up all your ingredients
    5. Bake in oven
