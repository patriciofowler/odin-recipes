Carnitas Tacos

Pork Carnitas Tacos

Carnitas tacos are slow cooked shredded pork tacos. They are cooking in a combination of spices, onion, garlic, and citrus fruits and juice. They make a delicious taco for your weekly taco rotation.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Orange
  • Lime
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cut pork shoulder into two inch pieces and add to large pot
  2. Cut orange into quarters and squeeze the juice into the pot and put orange in pot
  3. Add spices to pot
  4. Squeeze lime juice into pot
  5. Add two cups of water to the pot
  6. Cover the pot and cook in oven at 300*F for two and a half hours
  7. Once the pork is tender remove from pot and shred it
  8. Remove orange from the pot and discard
  9. Reduce remaining liquid in pot until about a cup is left
  10. Mix shredded pork with the liquid
  11. Spread the pork onto a foil lined pan and crisp the top layer under a broiler
  12. Serve the pork carnitas on warmed corn tortillas and top with your favorite toppings
